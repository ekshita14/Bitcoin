📌 Project Overview

The Bitcoin Market Intelligence Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze Bitcoin market performance from 2018 to 2025. The dashboard provides insights into price movements, trading activity, market volatility, bullish/bearish trends, and monthly returns, helping users understand historical market behavior and key performance indicators.

🎯 Objectives
Analyze Bitcoin price trends over time.
Track All-Time High (ATH) and All-Time Low (ATL) prices.
Monitor trading volume and total trade activity.
Evaluate Buy vs Sell market pressure.
Identify bullish and bearish market conditions.
Measure monthly returns and market growth.
Visualize volatility and price movement patterns.

🔍 Key Insights
Bitcoin achieved an ATH of approximately $126K during the analyzed period.
Strong correlation observed between trading volume and price surges.
Bullish and bearish periods were nearly balanced across the dataset.
Significant volatility spikes were observed during major market cycles.
Monthly returns highlight both high-growth periods and market corrections.
🚀 Project Outcome

This dashboard transforms raw cryptocurrency market data into actionable business intelligence, enabling investors, analysts, and crypto enthusiasts to explore Bitcoin's historical performance through interactive and visually engaging analytics.
